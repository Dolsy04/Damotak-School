CRECHE & NUSERY CLASS

Foundation Level

Empowering Young Minds to Shape a Radiant Future

At Damotak Academy, our Early Years Program, known as the Launchpad Level, is where young learners take their first steps toward a lifetime of success. We’ve crafted a unique, holistic curriculum that blends the finest elements of Nigerian educational systems, tailored to five progressive levels:

  • Tiny Explorers (Creche Class)
  • Little Pioneers (Nusery One Class)
  • Budding Innovators (Nusery Two Class)

Our program is designed to ignite curiosity and foster growth across key areas, including Literacy & Language Development, Numeracy & Problem-Solving Skills, Science & World Awareness, Social Skills & Emotional Intelligence, Creative Arts & Self-Expression, and Physical Development & Motor Skills. Every classroom is a vibrant hub of discovery, featuring specialized learning zones like reading corners, art studios, sensory play areas, nature discovery tables, and imaginative role-play spaces. At Damotak Academy, we don’t just teach—we inspire, empower, and prepare your child for a future full of possibilities.

CRECHE CLASS

At Damotak Academy, our Creche class, Tiny Explorers, is a warm and nurturing space designed for our youngest learners. Here, we focus on building a strong foundation through sensory play, early communication skills, and social interaction in a safe, stimulating environment. Our caring educators ensure every child feels loved, secure, and ready to explore the world around them.

NUSERY CLASS

At Damotak Academy, our nusery class, Little Pioneers & Budding Innovators, is a warm and nurturing space designed for our youngest learners. Here, we focus on building a strong foundation through sensory play, early communication skills, and social interaction in a safe, stimulating environment. Our caring educators ensure every child feels loved, secure, and ready to explore the world around them.
