SECONDARY CLASS

Junior Secondary Education

Year 7 to 9 (JSS 1 to 3)

Ages of 12 to 13

At Damotak School, our Junior Secondary program is designed to provide a seamless transition from primary education, fostering intellectual curiosity and personal growth. We blend the Nigerian curriculum with international standards to deliver a well-rounded education that emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Our approach nurtures cultural awareness, inclusivity, and the development of 21st-century skills, with a strong focus on communication, self-discipline, and respect.

Core subjects like Mathematics, English, Basic Science, and Social Studies are enriched with hands-on learning in Technology, Arts, and Physical Education. Through project-based activities, outdoor exploration, and co-curricular programs, we inspire students to explore their interests, develop problem-solving skills, and build confidence. At Damotak School, we are dedicated to preparing young adolescents for the challenges of Senior Secondary and beyond, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a dynamic world.

Subjects Junior Secondary Classes

  • Mathematics
  • Basic Technology
  • Basic Science
  • Social Studies
  • Physical and Health Education (PHE)
  • Cultural and Creative Art
  • Religious Studies(IRS / CRS)
  • Local Language (Yoruba)
  • Foreign Language (French)

  • Home Economics
  • Agricultural Science
  • Business Studies
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

Senior Secondary Education

Year 10 to 13 (SSS 1 to 3)

Ages of 14 to 16

At Damotak School, our Senior Secondary program is tailored to prepare students for higher education, vocational training, and life beyond the classroom. We offer a robust curriculum that combines the Nigerian curriculum with global educational standards, emphasizing academic excellence, innovation, and leadership. Our program fosters cultural awareness, inclusivity, and the development of 21st-century skills, with a strong focus on critical thinking, communication, and ethical responsibility.

Students engage in advanced studies in core subjects like Mathematics, English, and the Sciences, alongside elective subjects such as Economics, Literature, Technical Drawing, and Computer Science. Through practical experiments, creative projects, and co-curricular activities, we encourage students to think critically, solve problems, and express themselves confidently. At Damotak School, we are committed to empowering students to achieve their full potential, equipping them with the skills and values needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Subjects for Senior Secondary Classes

  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

  • Further Mathematics
  • Geography
  • Economics

  • History
  • Agricultural Science
  • Technical Drawing
  • Computer Science
  • Commerce
  • Finacial Accounting
